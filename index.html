How to choose colors?

Learn how to get your color palette in 4 steps.

1 Start with 2 neutral colors for the text and the background

2 Choose your primary and secondary colors. Primary is for main CTAs and sections, and Secondary is for less important buttons and info cards.

3 Accent color is an additional color. It can appear in images, highlights, hyperlinks, boxes, cards, etc.

4 Happy with the results? Press on “Export” and have fun with your color palette!
